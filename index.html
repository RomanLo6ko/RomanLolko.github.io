FAQ

Things people ask.

Is my family’s data private?
Completely. myDynasty is built privacy-first. Your family’s stories, photos, and documents are yours — not ours. We don’t sell data. We don’t mine it. We store it with end-to-end encryption.
How is myDynasty different from Ancestry or MyHeritage?
Those platforms are archives. myDynasty is a story. We don’t just help you find records — we help you understand the people behind them. Less database. More meaning.
Do I need to know a lot about my family to start?
Start with what you know. Even one name, one photo, one memory is enough. myDynasty grows with your family’s story — there’s no minimum.
Can multiple family members contribute?
Yes — and that’s the point. Every family has different memory-keepers. myDynasty brings them together. Invite relatives, assign roles, build one shared story across generations.
What happens to our data if we stop using the app?
It’s yours. Always. Export everything — photos, stories, tree data — at any time, in full. We believe your family’s story should never be locked inside someone else’s platform.
