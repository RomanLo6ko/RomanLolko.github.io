FAQ

Things people ask.

What is myDynasty?
myDynasty is an early-access mobile app project for preserving family trees, photos, documents, biographies, and AI-assisted family stories.
Why do you ask for my email?
We use your email only to notify you about beta access, launch updates, or important availability news for myDynasty. We do not sell waitlist emails.
Is my family’s data private?
myDynasty is being built with privacy-first principles. Family data should belong to the family. The waitlist website only collects the information you submit through the waitlist or contact form.
How is myDynasty different from Ancestry or MyHeritage?
Those platforms are often focused on records and research. myDynasty is focused on memory: stories, photos, context, people, places, and the meaning behind the family tree.
Do I need to know a lot about my family to start?
Start with what you know. Even one name, one photo, or one memory is enough. myDynasty is designed to grow with your family’s story over time.
Are AI-generated stories always accurate?
No. AI-assisted stories should be treated as drafts. Family members should review, correct, and approve important details before saving or sharing them.
