1. About the app

myDynasty is a private, on-device family tree and family memory app. It lets you build a visual family tree, attach photos and documents, record biographies, map places and burial sites, and import or export your data. The app works locally on your device: it does not require an account, and it does not upload your family data to any cloud server operated by us.
