FAQ

Things people ask.

What is myDynasty?

A private, on-device mobile app for family trees, photos, documents, biographies, places and burial sites. Available on Google Play.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no sign-in, no email and no password. Your data stays on your device — nothing is uploaded to a cloud.

Is it free?

Yes, there is a free tier: 1 family tree, 1 gallery, up to 50 people and 25 archive files. Premium unlocks more space (up to 5 trees, 5 galleries, 500 people and 500 archive files) for EUR 4.99 per month.

Can I move my existing tree in?

Yes. Import from GEDCOM 7.0 or LRV, and export to GEDCOM, LRV, SVG or PNG at any time.
