Privacy-first direction

Your family story belongs to your family.

myDynasty is being designed around careful handling of personal memories, no selling family data, and clear export options.

  • Waitlist email is used only for beta or launch updates.
  • AI-assisted stories are drafts and should be reviewed by family members.
  • Optional personal or medical document notes are for personal record keeping only, not medical advice.
Not just records

A family archive should explain why each person mattered.

myDynasty is not trying to be another database. It is being built as a place where a tree, a biography, a photograph, a document and a story can finally belong together.

FAQ

Things people ask.

What is myDynasty?

An early-access mobile app project for preserving family trees, photos, documents, biographies and AI-assisted family stories.

Why do you ask for my email?

Only to notify you about beta access, launch updates or important availability news. Waitlist emails are not sold.

Are AI-generated stories always accurate?

No. They should be treated as drafts. Family members should review, correct and approve important details before saving or sharing them.

Is the app already available?

Not yet. Private beta is planned for 2026. The waitlist is for early access notifications.
